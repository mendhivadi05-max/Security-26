Prohibited Conduct

  • Accessing data without authorization or sharing credentials.
  • Using member data for unrelated personal, commercial, political, or abusive purposes.
  • Sending spam, harassment, threats, deceptive messages, or messages outside approved templates.
  • Uploading or entering false, discriminatory, offensive, or unlawful content.
  • Attempting to bypass authentication, rate limits, bot checks, or platform restrictions.
  • Scraping, bulk exporting, or copying records unless authorized for club operations.

Messaging Rules

WhatsApp messages must be sent only to members who should receive the communication, using approved templates, accurate variables, and appropriate timing.

Incident Reporting

Users should promptly report suspected account compromise, incorrect records, unauthorized access, or inappropriate platform use to the administrator.

Enforcement

Violations may result in message blocking, record correction, access removal, disciplinary review, or other appropriate action.
